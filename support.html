Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > After, then tap Cancel Subscription.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your journal entries, check-ins, and personal data are stored locally on your device only. We never see or store your personal information on any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete All Data." You can also uninstall the app to remove all data from your device.

Can I change my theme?

Yes! Go to Settings and tap "Theme" to switch between Bloom, Glow Up, Forge, and Summit at any time.

What if I'm in crisis?

After is a self-improvement app and is not a substitute for professional help. If you are in crisis, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline by calling or texting 988, or reach out to a licensed mental health professional.
