Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

After ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the After mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Data you provide: When you use After, you may enter personal information such as mood ratings, journal entries, daily check-in responses, unsent letters, and vision board prompts. This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Subscription data: If you subscribe to After Premium, payment is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

2. Data Storage

All personal data you enter into After (check-ins, journal entries, letters, mood data, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your data stays on your iPhone.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information through the app
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs

4. Third-Party Services

Apple App Store: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

AI Image Generation: If you use the Vision Board feature with AI image generation, your text prompt (not personal data) may be sent to a third-party AI service (fal.ai) to generate images. No personal information from your check-ins, journal, or profile is shared with this service.

5. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete All Data" option in the app's Settings
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

6. Children's Privacy

After is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date on this page.
